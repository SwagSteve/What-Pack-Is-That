FixyText

Quick Run With Steam

Info


In Pack: 10

Player Count: 3-8


Average Time: 15 Minutes


Audience Capacity: Up To 10,000

In FixyText, players are thrown into a delightful machine to answer someone else’s important messages for them. Think of yourselves as script doctors, but for texts!
First, you decide on one of four conversation types: Flirty, Serious Business, Friends & Fam, or Unknown Number. This sets which chat partners and wacky requests are sent your way. Then, groups of players will take turns writing (or, rather, rewriting) and voting on replies to some of the craziest chat partners this side of…well, your phone!
When the timer starts, everyone in your group will jump into the same text field and start writing. Get in each other's way, change each other’s words, and get the most votes from other players by making your words stand out! Oh, and one last thing: You can’t backspace OR delete. So everything you type will stay that way FOREVER!
Extra features include:

- Collab bonuses for working on the same word
- Wacky suggestions to take your texts to the next level
- Doubling down on your votes
- No-timer mode
- Customizable characters
- A super bopping soundtrack!

If you’re a streamer and like to keep your reins on the chaos, maintain order in your stream with updated moderation tools including the ability to kick rambunctious players mid-game. An audience of thousands can play along and vote for their favorite words.
