Tee K.O. 2

Quick Run With Steam

Info


In Pack: 10

Player Count: 3-8


Average Time: 20 Minutes


Audience Capacity: Up To 10,000

DRAW. WRITE. CRAFT. FIGHT!

Welcome back to Tee Shirt Island. Unless you’ve never been here before, in which case, welcome… and prepare to rock.

In TKO2, a new cast of characters engage in the timeless art of fashion battle. Players create drawings and slogans – the weirder, the better – then mix a drawing and slogan to forge a clothing item never before seen.

BATTLE TIME! Everyone’s fresh new garments go head-to-head in the battle arena. Vote for your favorite… and watch the competition get knocked senseless.

Players can switch things up in later rounds by retooling drawings to increase their odds in battle… leading to a frantic button-mashing final round, where one garment emerges victorious and the loser literally goes down in flames.

DID WE MENTION:

- New design options to craft the perfect warrior! New shirt and drawing colors, new drawing tools, new slogan styles, and a choice of shirt, hoodie or tank top for every design.
- Love it? WEAR IT! Every new design in the game available in real life in multiple styles, to wear with reckless abandon and remind the world you’re not kidding around.
- Audience involvement: up to 10,000 audience members vote on battle contenders & help decide the victor!
- Avatars from across the Jackbox world: always wanted to see the Drawful owl annihilate its enemies? Now you can. (Also: are you okay?)

Tee K.O. 2 brings you all the laughs of doodling and writing with friends, all the drama of a WWE match, and all the surprising fashion of an Italian runway. Feeling crafty and competitive? Then BRING IT.
