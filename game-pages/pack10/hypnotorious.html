Hypnotorious

Quick Run With Steam

Info


In Pack: 10

Player Count: 4-8


Average Time: 20 Minutes


Audience Capacity: Up To 10,000

HYPNOSIS, TEAMWORK AND HIDDEN IDENTITIES!!! In Hypnotorious, players are attending a hypnotist’s show, where, as you might expect, they get hypnotized. Each player will take on a new, unique identity such as Frankenstein, a snowman or a carrot. They will then be asked to answer questions as their character would.

Is that it? Hardly. The characters have secret connections that must be uncovered. Some might fit together because they’re monsters or famous celebrities or because they always wear green. It will be up to you and your fellow players to deduce the secret categories and group yourselves based upon your suspicions.

Ready for a final twist? There is one player that is in a group all alone. They are The Outlier! They might seem like they fit in, but if you really pay attention to the clues, you might find that The Outlier’s answers don’t quite work with everyone else’s. After three questions, everyone will get the chance to declare who they think the Outlier is. Get it right, and you score big points. Get it wrong, and the Outlier takes all those points for their lonesome. And did we mention that even The Outlier doesn’t know they’re The Outlier? It could even be you!

So, watch what you say, place your trust in others wisely and above all else… enjoy the show!
