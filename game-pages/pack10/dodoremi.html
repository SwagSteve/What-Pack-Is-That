Dodo Re Mi

Quick Run With Steam

Info


In Pack: 10

Player Count: 1-9


Average Time: 5 Minutes


Audience Capacity: Up To 10,000

Dodo Re Mi is a musical game where you play as these incredible birds, select songs and instruments to play, and then, listen to a playback of your performance and witness your awesome success (or just as awesome utter failure).

Here are some exciting features: Your device is your instrument. As you play, you will hear the music your instrument is playing coming out of your controller! There is a sync up moment before playing a song to make sure the game is aligned with your controller. So the game is built to play in the room together or over streaming! (Audio latency, get out of here!) You determine how many songs you want to play in a game! Whether you are a one-hit wonder or playing a festival length playlist, end the game when you want. Players can join or drop out in between songs! The song list has some locked content that you open by playing! But if that’s not your thing, there’s a toggle in the settings that lets you unlock everything. The song list includes reimagined classics, original songs, and Jackbox favorites. And because each song contains multiple beatmaps to play, there are over 200 beatmaps! Each song contains a selection of instruments that have different beatmaps, ranging in difficulty and covering different musical parts! There are over 100 instruments to play, including Constant Screaming and Nature’s Bugle! What’s a beatmap? The thing on your device that you tap with your fingers to make that sweet, sweet music.

Birds of a feather can rock together in Dodo Re Mi!
