Nonsensory

Quick Run With Steam

Info


In Pack: 9

Player Count: 3-8


Average Time: 15-25 Minutes


Audience Capacity: Up To 10,000

Nonsensory is a silly, laid-back drawing, writing AND guessing game hoping to test your levels of NSP (Nonsensory Perception).

Can you make up a movie title that would be 80% likely to be the box office smash of the summer? Can you draw a puppy that’s only a 3 out of 10 on the adorableness scale?

More importantly, how close can you get to guessing the secret numbers the other players were given in their prompts? The closer you get to the correct number the more points you receive. Getting close… even kinda close… is still worthwhile.

If you think your guess is super close, you can also hit Confidence to earn even more points… if you’re correct. But if you use Confidence and you’re too far away from the correct spot on the range you’ll lose points instead.

The final round takes things to an even more absurd level asking players to draw strange combinations of two things. Could you a draw something that was halfway between a skull and a piece of sporting equipment? Could you draw something that was mostly rock star but also a little bit priest? Or something that’s both roller coaster and toilet but still convey that it’s just slightly closer to being a toilet?

You’ll laugh at your friends’ ridiculous drawings but also be amazed by how well you’re able to intuit the subtle variations.

If you’re a streamer, your audience will be able to test their own ability to establish an extrasensory group mind by trying to sync up with the special Card Challenge during drawing and writing time.
